The Signs

At first, those who do not explore music start to lose subtle expressions—smiles become less genuine, eyes lose their spark, and the large range of facial expressions diminishes.

This shift happens because music, which acts as an emotional guide, is no longer present. Without it, the face no longer reflects the full range of emotions, starting with subtle, almost imperceptible changes.

Just as an artist uses different colors to create depth in a painting, music provides the emotional "colors" that people need to express themselves fully. Without these colors, the canvas—the face—begins to blur.
